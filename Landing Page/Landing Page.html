Our Principles

Lazy Out harnesses the power of three sets of design principles, Simplicity, Gestalt and Usability, in order to achieve a friendlier interface for the user through the means of design.


Gestalt

- Similarity: both in design and in navigation.
- Proximity: the hub is designed to have the icons all snuggled up, as to not waste space.

- Organize: organizing the site and its navigation for a simpler use.
